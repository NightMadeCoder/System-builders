Since 1992

30 YEARS OF SUCCESS WORK

SYSTEM BUILDERS LIMITED was incoporated in the year 1991, with registration number RC N0. 179383. The company is engaged in civil/structural Engineering, General Procurement and supplies as its Principal business. Our head office is currently located at NO 4, Murtala square, Kaduna with a branch office at house N0.3, Ohinoyi street, Area 2, Abuja.

We have acquired over the years. expertise in general construction and civil engineering service to government, Parastatals, Agencies,Industries etc.
Our core business speecialties are civil/structural Engineering, Roads and Bridges Construction, buildings, earth Dams and power distribution, and general supplies.
